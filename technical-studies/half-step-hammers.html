Half Step Hammers

Objectives

Start by listening to the full exercise.

Next, work through the practice sections one by one.

Once you're comfortable with all the practice sections, you should have no problem playing through the full exercise.

Remember, you can use the settings icon on the video to adjust the playback speed.

Full Exercise

Listen first, then play along after practicing the other sections.

Section 1 Practice

Practice the orange notes.

Section 2 Practice

Practice the blue notes.

Section 3 Practice

Practice switching from the orange notes to the blues notes.

Section 4 Practice

Practice the pink notes.

Section 5 Practice

Practice connecting the blue notes to the pink notes.

Section 6 Practice

Practice connecting the pink notes to the blue notes.
