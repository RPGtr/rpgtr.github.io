Half-Step Hammers

Full Exercise

Work through sections 1-6, then play along here with the full exercise.

Section 1

First, practice only the orange group of notes.

Section 2

Second, practice the blue group of notes.

Section 3

Here you'll practice connecting the orange group to the blue group.

Section 4

Now the pink group of notes.

Section 5

Here you'll connect the blue group to the pink group.

Section 6

Now you'll reverse and go from pink to blue.
